About FrameForge

We're building the future of video editing — powerful tools that anyone can use, right in the browser.

Our Mission

FrameForge was founded with a simple belief: professional video editing shouldn't require expensive software or powerful hardware. We're democratizing content creation by putting studio-grade tools in the browser.

Our AI-powered editing suite helps creators of all levels produce stunning videos in minutes, not hours. From YouTube creators to marketing teams, FrameForge is the tool of choice for fast, beautiful video production.
