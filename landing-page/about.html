About NetMirror

We're on a mission to make premium entertainment accessible to everyone. NetMirror brings the world's best movies, series, and originals to your fingertips — beautifully designed, effortlessly streamed.

Our Story

NetMirror was born out of a simple idea: streaming should be simple, beautiful, and accessible. In a landscape of expensive subscriptions and fragmented content libraries, we saw an opportunity to create something different — a platform that puts the viewer first.

Founded in 2025, NetMirror has grown from a small passion project into a full-featured streaming platform serving users across India and beyond. Our team of engineers, designers, and content curators work tirelessly to bring you a Netflix-quality experience.

What We Offer

  • 10,000+ titles — Bollywood, Hollywood, Korean dramas, anime, and regional content
  • HD & 4K streaming — Adaptive quality that works on any connection speed
  • Dolby Audio — Immersive sound with multi-language audio tracks
  • Smart subtitles — 15+ languages with customizable appearance
  • Daily updates — New titles added every day, curated picks weekly
  • Lightweight app — Under 15 MB, optimized for all Android devices
