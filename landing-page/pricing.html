Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately and billing is adjusted proportionally.
Is there a free trial?
New users get a 7-day free trial on any plan. You won't be charged until the trial ends. Cancel anytime during the trial at no cost.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept UPI, credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay), net banking, and popular wallets through our secure Razorpay payment gateway.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime from your account settings in the app. Your access continues until the end of your current billing cycle. No cancellation fees.
Can I share my account?
Standard and Premium plans support multiple simultaneous streams, so your household can enjoy NetMirror together on different devices.
